## Playback

| Key         | Operation
| ----------- | ---------------------
| `Space`     | Play/pause
| `-`         | Speed: slower
| `=`         | Speed: faster
| `⌫`         | Speed: reset to 100%

## Navigation

| Key         | Operation
| ----------- | ---------------------
| `Right`     | Seek forward
| `Left`      | Seek backward
| `Down`      | Seek delta: reduce
| `Up`        | Seek delta: increase
| `,`         | Previous entity
| `/`         | Entity-type dropdown
| `.`         | Next entity

## Jump

| Key         | Operation
| ----------- | ---------------------
| `jj`        | By time
| `Enter`     | To start (contextual)
| `jh`        | Jump history...
| `jb`        | Backward
| `jf`        | Forward

## Scratch loop bounds {#nudge-bindings}

| Key         | Operation
| ----------- | ---------------------
| `[[`        | Start: set now
| `[⌫`        | Start: reset to video start
| `[-`        | Start: nudge: decrease
| `[=`        | Start: nudge: increase
| `[\`        | Start: edit
| `]]`        | End: set now
| `]⌫`        | End: reset to video end
| `]-`        | End: nudge: decrease
| `]=`        | End: nudge: increase
| `]\`        | End: edit
| `[]` · `][` | Nudge_delta: activate dropdown

## Scratch loop edit mode

| Key         | Operation
| ----------- | ---------------------
| `Tab`       | Toggle focus: start/end
| `Right`     | Increase: start/end
| `Left`      | Decrease: start/end
| `Up`        | Increase: nudge-delta
| `Down`      | Decrease: nudge-delta
| `Space`     | Play/pause near start/end
| `⌫`         | Reset: start/end
| `Enter`     | Exit mode
| `Esc`       | Exit mode
